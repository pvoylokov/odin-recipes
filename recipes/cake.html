Grandma's Chocolate Lemon Cake


my cake's not a lie!

    ingredients:
  • butter 130g + 200g
  • sugar 1 glass + 1 glass + 3 tbsp
  • flour 1 glass
  • cocoa 3 tsp + 4 tsp
  • baking soda 1 tsp
  • semolina 3 tbsp
  • milk 2 glass
  • eggs 3
  • sour cream 4 tbsp
  • lemons 2
  • note: a glass is 250 ml

    batter:
  1. add 130 g of melted butter to 1 glass of sugar (mix)
  2. add 3 eggs one by one
  3. add one glass of flour (240 g)
  4. add 1tsp of baking soda
  5. result: runny batter
  6. divide into two parts
  7. first half: put in a buttered dish
  8. bake @ 170-180 C about 20 min
  9. add 3 tsp of cocoa to the second half bake @ 170-180 C about 20 min
  10. divide each cake lengthwise into two (totaling four: two brown and two yellow)

    cream:
  1. make semolina gruel: 2 glasses of milk and 3 tbsp of semolina (no sugar or salt added)
  2. when it cools, add juice and zest from the lemons
  3. mix 200 g of butter with 1 glass of sugar
  4. add to gruel, mix well and cool in the fridge for about 30 min
  5. stack the cakes, putting the cream between each layer

    glaze:
  1. mix 4 tbsp of sour cream to 4 tsp of cocoa and 3 tbsp of sugar
  2. boil for 5 minutes, mixing thoroughly, then add a slice of butter and let reach boiling point
  3. top the cake with glaze from above
