Best Lasagna Ever


my lasagna's off the hook yo!

    ingredients:
  • feta
  • lasagne sheets
  • frozen spinach
  • tomato sauce

    steps:
  1. cut the feta and frozen spinach into small squares about 2 cm
  2. layer the feta + spinach in the pan
  3. layer sheets of lasagna above
  4. repeat the feta and spinach layer and then the lasagna sheets
  5. pour tomato sauce over the sheets
  6. cover w/ foil
  7. bake @ 180 C for about 30 minutes
